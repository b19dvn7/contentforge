Why Prompt Quality Matters

AI image generators interpret your prompt literally. "A dog in a park" might give you anything from a photorealistic golden retriever to a cartoon doodle on a bench. The more specific you are, the closer the result matches your vision.

A well-structured prompt includes:

  • Subject — What's in the image (be specific)
  • Medium — Photograph, illustration, 3D render, oil painting
  • Style — Minimalist, hyperrealistic, cyberpunk, watercolor
  • Lighting & Color — Golden hour, neon, moody, pastel
  • Composition — Close-up, wide angle, bird's eye view
  • Mood — Peaceful, dramatic, mysterious, energetic

Platform-Specific Prompt Optimization

Each AI image platform has its own prompt language. What works in Midjourney doesn't work as well in DALL-E. ContentForge's prompt generator tailors output for:

Midjourney

Midjourney responds well to style keywords, aspect ratios, and parameter flags. A good Midjourney prompt includes --ar 16:9, --v 6, and style modifiers like cinematic lighting or octane render. The AI prompt generator includes these automatically.

DALL-E 3

DALL-E handles natural language well but benefits from clear structure. It's particularly good at following detailed descriptions of composition and style. The generator emphasizes descriptive clarity for DALL-E prompts.

Stable Diffusion

Stable Diffusion uses prompting similar to Midjourney but with more emphasis on quality tags like masterpiece, best quality, and negative prompts. The generator includes quality boosters and can suggest negative prompts.
