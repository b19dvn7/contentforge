What to Look for in a Free AI Writing Tool

Not all free AI writing tools are created equal. Here's what to consider:

  • Output quality — Does the tool generate coherent, useful text, or is it gibberish?
  • Customization options — Can you adjust tone, length, and format?
  • No hidden limits — Some "free" tools cap you at 5 uses before pushing a paid plan
  • Privacy — What happens to the text you generate? Is it used for training?
  • Speed — Good AI writing should return results in seconds, not minutes

How to Get the Best Results

AI writing tools work best when you give them clear, specific prompts. Here are some tips:

  1. Be specific about your audience. Instead of "write a social post," try "write a LinkedIn post for B2B SaaS founders about content marketing."
  2. Specify the tone. Professional, casual, urgent, friendly — the AI can adjust if you tell it how.
  3. Include keywords. For SEO content, include the keywords you want to target in your prompt.
  4. Iterate. Generate multiple variations and pick the best one, or combine elements from each.
  5. Edit the output. AI-generated text is a starting point, not a finished product. Always review and refine.
