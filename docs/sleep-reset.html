The Three Root Causes of Bad Sleep

  1. Confused circadian rhythm — Artificial light after sunset tricks your brain into thinking it's daytime
  2. Overactive nervous system — Constant stimulation keeps you in fight-or-flight mode
  3. Bad sleep environment — Temperature, light, and noise levels prevent deep sleep

The 7-Day Protocol

Days 1-2: Foundation Reset

  • Morning light within 30 minutes of waking
  • Consistent wake/sleep schedule
  • Breakfast within 1 hour of waking
  • Dim lights 2 hours before bed
  • Screens off 1 hour before bed

Days 3-4: Environment Overhaul

  • Room temperature 65-68°F (18-20°C)
  • Complete darkness audit — cover every LED
  • Block light under door with towel
  • White noise or earplugs for consistent sound

Days 5-6: Wind-Down System

The 60-minute countdown:

  • T-60: Screens off
  • T-45: Light stretching or warm shower
  • T-30: Worry download — write down 3 things
  • T-15: Get in bed with a physical book
  • T-0: Lights out

Day 7+: Maintenance

Daily checklist to keep your sleep on track:

  • Morning light within 30 min
  • Breakfast within 1 hour
  • Screens off 1 hour before bed
  • Room dark and cool
  • Worry download completed
